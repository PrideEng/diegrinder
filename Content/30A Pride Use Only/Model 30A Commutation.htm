Model 30A Commutation

  1. Install shot pin in grinder.

  2. Open Engineering.

  1. Right click IndraMotion_MTX_standard_L45. Click Switch online.

  1. Navigate to Logic > POU’s. Open prAxes.

  1. Scroll down to fbAxis[C].

  1. Click on the line after Drive_Enable.

  1. Right click, select Insert Contact.

  1. Type FALSE, press Enter.

  1. New FALSE contact has now been added.

  1. Navigate to Logic > POU’s. Open prAxesHandling.

  1. Scroll down to iAxis07_C.iAx_DrvAct.

  1. Right click, select Insert Contact Parallel (below).

  1. Type TRUE, press Enter.

  1. New TRUE contact has now been added.

  1. Click Build > Clean.

  1. Click Yes.

  1. Click Build > Build.

  1. Click Debug > Login.

  1. Click Yes.

  1. Click Yes.

  1. Click Debug > Start.

  1. Click Yes.

  1. Turn on drive power on the VAM. Note the change in the ladder logic. Drive Power light on VAM will flash with C axis disabled.

  1. Navigate to Sercos. Click + to expand and display the drives.

  1. Click on drive 7, C Axis. Drive will switch from grey to blue. Only need to switch drive 7 online for C commutation.

  1. Expand HCS01.1 [7] Caxis > Axis [7] Caxis > Drive Control > Motor Control. Double click Commutation settings.

  1. Commutation settings. Use Saturation method.

  1. Only use initial commissioning mode if setting up a new grinder.

  1. Click No if you receive this popup message. Do NOT activate automatic backward movement.

  1. Click the tab labeled Saturation method.

  1. Delete the existing values in test signal. Set Amplitude and Frequency to 0.

  1. Click Determine commutation offset. The C axis will make an audible noise when this occurs.

  1. Amplitude and Frequency will automatically be filled in by the grinder. Close the Commutation settings window.

  1. Click Debug > Logout.

  1. Open prAxesHandling. Select and delete the TRUE contact.

  1. Open prAxes. Select and delete the FALSE contact.

  1. Click Build > Clean.

  1. Click Yes.

  1. Click Build > Build.

  1. Click Debug > Login.

  1. Click Yes.

  1. Click Yes.

  1. Click Debug > Start.

  1. Click Yes. The commutation process is now complete.
