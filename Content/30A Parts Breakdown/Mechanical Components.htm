Mechanical Components

Air Purification and Filtration System

This multi-stage filtration system insures long-lasting air bearings. The system removes oil, oil mist,

water and vapor down to .001 PPM (.01micron). To maintain air bearing performance the supply of

filtered air must be left on 24 hours a day.
