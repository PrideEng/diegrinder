Work Head Positioning , and Axis

  1. At the opening screen select "Grinder Set Up".

  2. Turn key to Man  mode. The display will show "Manual"  at the top of the screen.

  3. Load 6 inch tooling ball fixture (19-127) into chuck. If using a 6 Jaw Chuck, move ahead to step 6. Energize magnetic chuck to 25%.

  4. Indicate tooling ball radially to within 0.0001 inches (2.5 Âµm) or better.

  5. Turn magnetic chuck on to full power.

  6. Move the indicator 0.5 inches (12.7mm) away from the tooling ball to serve as a visual reference while aligning the swing point of the tooling ball.

  7. Manually operate the axis through 90 degree limits, and move the and work head slides until the ball looks visually centered.

  8. As the tooling ball gets closer to being centered, move the indicator closer to the ball. Finally, the indicator can be moved to contact the tooling ball when the and adjustments are visually close. Be careful with the indicator. Once the indicator is in contact with the ball you are ready for final adjustments.

  9. At this time it is important that the indicator tip be set at center height of the tooling ball and 90 degrees to the direction which the tooling ball is to be rotated.

  10. Continue to Jog the and work head slides until the indicator does not move when rotating the axis through 90 degrees of travel.

  11. You are now on center with the axis and exactly 0.5000 inches (12.7mm) from the face of the chuck. You are ready to set zero accordingly.

  12. Select the maintenance icon at the lower right of the screen.

  13. Select the icon at the lower left hand of the screen.

  14. Turn the key tomode.

  15. Select the Axis by touching the gray icon on the right side of the screen. Notice that the of the Axis resets to zero automatically after the is selected. Select . Select the axis by touching the gray icon . Select the icon, it will automatically reset the Axis to zero. Select . You have now completed re-setting the tooling ball. If you are using a 6/7 inch step jaw chuck, continue on with the following steps:

  16. Load the 7 Inch Tooling Ball (9-185) in the 7 Inch jaws.

  17. Maintain the axis position at the zero position set in Step 15 and the axis at 0 degrees.

  18. Select the Axis so that you can observe the current position and move the Axis in the (-) direction approximately 0.600 inches (15.24 mm).

  19. Place the indicator tip on the tooling ball side closest to the operator.

  20. Rotate the axis manually through 90 degrees and observe the indicator. Move the Axis until the indicator remains within +/- 0.0001 inches (0.0025mm).

  21. Record the Y Axis current position value. This value will be used in any 7 Inch Re-Draw Die programs window.
